Privacy Policy

SubSlayer Subscription Tracker — Last updated: April 22, 2026

SubSlayer Subscription Tracker ("the App") is a personal subscription tracking tool developed by Mussta.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information you enter (subscription names, prices, billing dates) is stored locally on your device only.

Third Parties

The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking services.

Notifications

The App uses local notifications to remind you of upcoming subscription billing dates. These notifications are generated on-device and no data leaves your device.
